GA4-visible sessions explained

What GA4-visible sessions mean in a controlled traffic testing context.

Explain that visibility in analytics is a measurement signal, not a guarantee of rankings or conversions.

Checklist

  1. Define the target page, date range, and campaign settings before the test.
  2. Confirm GA4 Realtime receives sessions during a controlled test window.
  3. Compare country, device, source/referrer, and page-path reports against expectations.
  4. Document screenshots, limitations, and follow-up checks before interpreting results.
